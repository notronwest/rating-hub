Comparing the three

  • D — densest, most table-like. Every field has its own column. Best if you value scan-ability across many items.
  • E — most "card-like". Player is the headline; time + type fade into a third line. Feels less tabular, more narrative.
  • F — headline is player, but the right rail is a consistent "slip" showing shot type + time + chips. Middle ground between D's table and E's card.
